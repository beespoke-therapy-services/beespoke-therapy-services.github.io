Services

I offer a range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each child. I offer a full speech and language assessment and tailored treatment to children who may present with any of the following:

  • Social communication difficulties
  • Neurodivergent children who may have differences in social communication skills, emotion regulation and attention and listening.
  • Unclear speech
  • Articulation difficulties
  • Speech sound awareness/phonics difficulties
  • Language delay/late talkers
  • Non-verbal children
  • Children struggling to identify and express their feelings
  • Difficulties with receptive language skills
  • Children who are Gestalt language processors
