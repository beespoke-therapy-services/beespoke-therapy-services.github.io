Fees & Pricing

Speech and Language Therapy

  • Full communication assessment: £350 (includes report)
  • Speech and language therapy session: £96 (includes therapy plus associated preparation and admin)
  • Multi disciplinary meeting or meeting of other type: £80 per hour
  • Travel (if required): mileage at 50p per mile + £20 per hour for travel time

Occupational Therapy, Mental Health Nursing and other services

Fees for other services will be provided upon request, as they may vary based on the specific needs of the client and the nature of the service required. Please contact us directly on beespoketherapy@gmail.com to discuss your requirements and receive a personalised quote.

Cancellation Policy

Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a session. This allows me sufficient time to offer the appointment slot to another client. Cancellations or non-attendance with less than 24 hours notice will be charged at the full session rate.

Late payment policy

All invoices must be paid within 14 days of receipt. Failure to make payment within this period may result in additional charges, including interest and any reasonable costs incurred in the recovery of outstanding amounts. We reserve the right to take further action to recover unpaid debts.
